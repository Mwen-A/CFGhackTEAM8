We are looking for a competitive, young and decisive salesman to join our team. This individual must have strong communication skills and he/she must be able to work well in a team.
Requirements

    A recent 2.1 undergraduate degree

    At least 1 year experience


Benefits

    Maternity pay

    Frequent work socials with bottles of champagne!

Your analysis

8 hits

Competitive

This is an example of male coded language. Have you considered "motivated" instead?

Young

This is an example of ageist language and older candidates could be put off applying. Have you considered "energetic" instead?

Salesman

Many gendered job titles still exist. Have you considered using "salesperson" to be more inclusive?

He/She

This is an example of LGTBQ+ unconscious bias. Using "they" instead would be more inclusive of gender identities.

Recent

This is an example of ageist language and older candidates could be put off applying. Is this word necessary?

Degree

We noticed that you asked for a degree: this could exclude candidates who do not have a degree but have previous experience, apprenticeship or bootcamp.

Maternity pay

This phrase could exclude candidates who may need to take time off for child caring responsibilities but are not a "mother". Have you considered "parental time off" or "having a child"?

Champagne

Mentions of alcohol could discourage candidates who do not drink. Instead, why not focus on the social aspects of work socials?
